The Project

Bringing Woodstock to the Farm.

Day N Mayfield was conceived as Stanford's answer to a genuine music festival: not a concert, not a club night, but a full-day outdoor experience rooted in the spirit of Woodstock. The vision — Mayfield as a community gathering place, with live music stages, a Shakedown Street vendor market, and the kind of joyful, spontaneous energy that Woodstock mythologized.

Signature Feature

Shakedown Street.

Inspired by the famous vendor market that followed the Grateful Dead on tour, Shakedown Street is a curated outdoor market running alongside the main stage: local vendors, student creators, and artisan goods creating an immersive festival atmosphere beyond just the music.
